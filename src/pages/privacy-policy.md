## Data we collect

We collect email addresses when you subscribe to our newsletter via the form on our website. These are stored and managed by Beehiiv.

## How we use it

Your email address is used only to send you the Seydisfjordur Community Radio newsletter. We will never sell or share your data with third parties.

## Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. The live chat (provided by RadioCult) and the audio player may set technical cookies necessary for their operation.

## Contact

To unsubscribe or request deletion of your data, email us at [hello@seydisfjordur.radio](mailto:hello@seydisfjordur.radio).
